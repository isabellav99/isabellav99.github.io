I'm Isabella.

And storytelling, for me, is as primal a need as eating. I began telling stories in the second grade when I wrote my first "novel" in a fuzzy blue notebook. As a teenage I took up theater, portraying life through song and dance. I want to be a storyteller 'when I grow up,' and I'm eager to discover exactly how my passion will turn into a career.
